About Us

At Santa Fe Construction, we partner with homeowners to achieve their dream home through our full-service remodeling expertise. Our specialties include interior and exterior design, room additions, kitchen and bathroom remodels, outdoor kitchens, stonework, concrete patios, and retaining walls. Our top priority is always providing exceptional service to our clients during the design and development process.

Our Process

Consultation

At SFC, our professionals ask detailed questions to capture your design preferences and translate them into a tangible plan.

Plan

After reviewing the estimate and signing the contract, we create a job and payment schedule mindful of your budget.

Build

Our skilled professionals prioritize quality and efficiency while keeping you updated through regular meetings during the project.
